Help & User Guide

Everything you need to know about navigating and using this website

Getting Around

How to Navigate the Website

This site has 5 pages. Use the top navigation bar to move between them on any device.

🏠

Home Page

The starting point of the website. It introduces Lingua World and all available languages.

1

Read the welcome message and discover which languages we offer.

2

Click "Book a Free Trial" to go directly to the booking form.

3

Scroll down to see course cards and benefits of studying at Lingua World.

🖼️

Gallery & Data Page

View language program images and browse the full course schedule table.

1

Browse the gallery cards — each one shows a different language program.

2

Scroll down to the schedule table to find timing, price, and availability.

3

Use the External Resources links to explore free online learning tools.

📝

Book a Class Page

Register your interest in a language course by completing the booking form.

1

Fill in your personal details — name, email, and phone are required.

2

Choose your language, level, and preferred schedule from the dropdown menus.

3

Select your gender using the radio buttons and tick any hobbies that apply.

4

Add any comments about your learning goals, then click "Submit My Booking".

Forms Guide

How to Use the Booking Form

Detailed instructions for filling in each part of the registration form correctly.

✏️

Text Input Fields

Fields like Name, Email, and Phone require you to type information directly.

1

Click on the field — it will highlight with a blue border showing it's active.

2

Type your information. Fields with * are required and cannot be left blank.

3

If you enter something wrong (e.g., invalid email), a red error will appear.

📋

Dropdown (Select) Fields

Used for Major, Level, Language, and Schedule — choose one option from a list.

1

Click the dropdown arrow to open the list of options.

2

Click the option that best describes you.

3

The field will close and your selection will be displayed.

🔘

Radio Buttons (Gender)

Radio buttons let you select exactly one option from a group.

1

You will see three gender options displayed as pill-shaped buttons.

2

Click the one that applies to you — only one can be selected at a time.

☑️

Checkboxes (Hobbies)

Checkboxes let you select multiple options at the same time.

1

Click any hobby that applies to you — a checkmark will appear inside.

2

You can select as many hobbies as you like, or none at all.

3

Click a checked box again to uncheck it.

💬

Text Area (Comments)

A large text box for writing your language learning goals or special requests.

1

Click inside the box and type your message freely.

2

You can drag the bottom-right corner to resize the text area.

3

Maximum 500 characters. This field is optional.

Gallery & Tables Guide

Understanding Images & Tables

🖼️

Gallery Images

Each gallery card represents one language program offered at Lingua World.

1

Hover over a card to see it lift with a scale effect — this is just visual feedback.

2

Read the caption below each image for a description of the class.

3

All images include descriptive text (alt text) for screen readers.

📊

Course Schedule Table

The data table on the Gallery page shows all course details in an organized grid.

1

The dark blue header row shows column titles — Language, Level, Schedule, etc.

2

Hover over any row to highlight it — this makes it easier to read across the row.

3

The colored badges in the Status column show: Green = Open, Yellow = Limited seats, Blue = Starting Soon.

4

On mobile, scroll left/right to see all table columns.

🔗

External Links

The Gallery page includes links to trusted external language learning websites.

1

Click any link card — external sites open in a new browser tab for safety.

2

Hover over a link to see it lift and gain a blue border highlight.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is this website mobile-friendly?

Yes! The website is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktop computers. The navigation adapts to smaller screens automatically.

❓ What happens after I submit the booking form?

Our team will review your registration and contact you within 24 hours via your provided email or phone number to confirm your class schedule.

❓ Can I register for more than one language?

Yes! Simply submit the booking form once for each language course you wish to join. Our team will coordinate the schedules to avoid conflicts.
